Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Austria / GDPR · stdksupport@proton.me

The short version

DashAI runs entirely on your device. Your conversations and images never reach our servers. We don't have a backend that receives your chats.

What we collect

Nothing you type or say. All AI inference happens locally via WebGPU in your browser.

Model files are downloaded once from Hugging Face CDN. Their servers log standard access data (IP, timestamp). See HuggingFace Privacy Policy.

Ads: We display Google AdSense ads. Google may use cookies to personalise ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com. See Google Privacy Policy.

Local storage: Your chosen model ID and settings are saved in your browser's localStorage. This never leaves your device.

Cookies

We set no first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies. You can block them in your browser settings.

Your GDPR rights

Under GDPR (EU 2016/679) you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Since we hold no conversational data, these rights mainly apply to AdSense data (contact Google) and support emails. To exercise a right, email stdksupport@proton.me. You may also file a complaint with the Austrian DSB: www.dsb.gv.at.

Copyright / Takedown

If you believe any content associated with DashAI infringes your rights, email stdksupport@proton.me — we will investigate and remove it as soon as possible. Please include a description of the material and your contact details.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. This page always shows the current version.
