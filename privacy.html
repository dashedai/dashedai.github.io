1. Who We Are

DashAI is a browser-based AI assistant operated as an independent web project. The service is based in Austria, European Union, and is therefore subject to EU data protection law including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). DashAI is delivered as a static web application — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files — with no back-end server or cloud infrastructure operated by us.

For any privacy-related enquiries, contact us at stdksupport@proton.me.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. DashAI does not collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (there are no accounts)
  • Your conversation messages or chat history
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Device identifiers, browser fingerprints, or hardware information
  • Usage statistics, session data, or behavioral analytics
  • Any cookies initiated by us (see Section 6 for third-party cookies)

3. How the Application Works

DashAI uses WebLLM, an open-source library that runs large language model inference entirely within your browser tab using the WebGPU API. When you select a model:

  • The model weights are downloaded from jsDelivr CDN or Hugging Face (see Section 5) directly to your browser's cache
  • All AI inference happens locally on your GPU — no request is ever sent to any AI API or cloud service
  • Conversation history exists only in your browser's RAM for the duration of the session
  • When you close the tab, all conversation data is gone permanently

Your conversation content is never transmitted anywhere — not to us, not to any AI provider, not to any third party.

4. Open-Weight AI Models & Hugging Face

DashAI uses open-weight AI models (specifically Meta's Llama family) distributed via the WebLLM project. "Open-weight" means the model parameters are publicly released and can be run by anyone on their own hardware — there is no central inference API.

Model files may be served from Hugging Face infrastructure (huggingface.co) as part of the WebLLM distribution network. We do not control Hugging Face's data practices. When your browser downloads model weights, Hugging Face may log standard CDN access data such as your IP address, browser user agent, and the file requested. This is governed by Hugging Face's Privacy Policy.

Importantly: once the model file is cached in your browser, no further requests are made to Hugging Face or any external server during your conversations.

5. Local Storage

DashAI uses your browser's localStorage for one purpose only: remembering which AI model you last selected, so you don't have to re-choose it on your next visit. This data:

  • Contains only the model identifier string (e.g., Llama-3.2-1B-Instruct-q4f16_1-MLC)
  • Never leaves your device
  • Can be cleared at any time via your browser settings or the DashAI Settings panel
  • Is not a cookie, tracking pixel, or any form of cross-site identifier

7. Third-Party Services

DashAI loads the following external resources. Each has its own privacy policy and may log standard CDN or web server access data:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — web fonts. Privacy Policy
  • jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — WebLLM library and model weights. Privacy Policy
  • Hugging Face (huggingface.co) — AI model weight distribution. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — Prism.js syntax highlighting library. Privacy Policy
  • unpkg.com — React and Babel JavaScript libraries.
  • Google AdSense — advertising network (see Section 8).

After initial page load and model download, DashAI operates entirely offline. None of these services receive your conversation content at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

DashAI is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in jurisdictions that require a higher age threshold for digital consent, including certain EU member states). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since DashAI collects no information from any user, no special provisions are required — but we state this explicitly for compliance with COPPA, the GDPR, and similar regulations.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

As DashAI is operated from Austria (EU), EU data protection law applies. Because we process no personal data about you, the practical exercise of most GDPR rights (access, erasure, portability, rectification) results in the same answer: there is no data about you for us to provide, delete, or export.

However, you retain the following rights in relation to data held by third-party services (Google, Hugging Face, etc.) that may have logged your IP or set cookies — please contact those services directly.

To delete your locally stored model preference (the only data DashAI stores), clear localStorage for this site in your browser settings, or use the "Clear all conversations" option in DashAI Settings.

For any questions, contact stdksupport@proton.me. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde, www.dsb.gv.at).
