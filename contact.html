Before you write
My model download is stuck / keeps restarting
Make sure you're on Chrome 113+ or Edge 113+. Check that WebGPU is enabled at chrome://flags/#enable-unsafe-webgpu. Large models (DashPro, ~5 GB) require a stable connection for the initial download.
Does DashAI work on Safari or Firefox?
Not yet — WebGPU support is incomplete in Safari and not yet available in Firefox. Use Chrome or Edge for the full experience.
I want to delete my data
DashAI stores only your last-used model ID in localStorage. Clear it via your browser settings, or use the Settings → Clear All option inside the app. We hold no data on our end — see the Privacy Policy.
How do I opt out of ads / personalised advertising?
Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad personalisation settings, or use aboutads.info.
