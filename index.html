FAQ

Common questions.

Does DashAI really work offline?add
Yes. After the model is downloaded and cached by your browser, DashAI requires zero internet. You can disconnect and it keeps working. The model lives in your browser's cache storage, not on any server.
What browsers are supported?add
DashAI requires WebGPU support. Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, and Chrome on Android (experimental) work well. Safari and Firefox do not yet support WebGPU fully.
Is my data stored anywhere?add
No. Your conversation history lives only in your browser's memory for the session. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics. The app is a static HTML file with no back-end whatsoever.
What is WebGPU?add
WebGPU is a modern web standard that gives browsers direct access to your GPU for high-performance computation. DashAI uses it to run AI model inference entirely on your hardware — the same way native apps do, but inside a browser tab.
Is DashAI free?add
Yes, DashAI is completely free. There's no subscription, no usage limits, and no premium tier. The AI runs on your hardware, so there's no cost to serve you.
How does the "Open links" feature work?add
When you ask Dash to open a website, it detects your intent and either shows a clickable button or opens the URL automatically (if Auto-open is enabled in settings). It uses a proprietary command syntax that the DashAI client parses before displaying the response.
